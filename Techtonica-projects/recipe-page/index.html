Mom's Awesome Spaghetti

My recipe project for Techtonica is inspired by a trip I took to Colombia last month. While there, I ate some of the best food of my entire life. I visited my friend's family in a small town called Acacias, where I had TRUE farm to table meals. I tried my first Colombian empanadas in Acacias, made with rice and meat, but when I traveled to the capitol, Bogota, I tried empanadas made with potatoes and beef. 10/10 would recommend! 😍

  • 2Tbs. olive oil
  • 1 green bell pepper, chopped
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 lb. ground turkey
  • 1 jar of spaghetti sauce
  • 1 box of spaghetti noodles

Make the spaghetti sauce

  1. Heat the olive oil in a large pan over medium-heat.
  2. Once the oil is hot, add the green bell pepper and onion, and sautee until they are soft.
  3. Add the ground turkey to the pan and pan-fry until it is cooked through, stirring occasionally, about 15 minutes.
  4. Once the ground turkey is cooked through, pour in the jar of spaghetti sauce. Reduce the heat to low, stir to combine, and let simmer.

Make the spaghetti noodles

  1. Heat a large pot of water on high heat, until boiling.
  2. Once boiling, add the box of spaghetti noodles and cook following the package's directions (usually 9-12 minutes).
